# ET AI News Platform — Business Impact Model

**PS8 submission — ET AI Hackathon 2026**

All figures are conservative estimates based on publicly available market data.
Conversion rates of 1–2% are used throughout to avoid overstating impact.

---

## Feature 1: Vernacular Engine

**Problem:** ET has 50M+ monthly readers but publishes in English only. 70% of India's internet users prefer regional languages (IAMAI India Internet Report 2025). This is a direct revenue gap — ET cannot monetise readers who cannot read English.

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| ET monthly readers | 50,000,000 |
| Regional-language preference (IAMAI 2025) | 70% |
| Addressable new readers | **35,000,000** |
| Conservative 2% conversion to ET Prime | 700,000 new subscribers |
| ET Prime price | ₹999 / year |
| **New ARR** | **₹69.9 Cr** |
| Translation cost (500 articles/day × $0.02) | $10/day → ₹3L/year |
| **Net impact** | **~₹70 Cr ARR at <1% of revenue in API costs** |

---

## Feature 2: Personalised Feed

**Problem:** The same homepage is served to every ET reader regardless of whether they cover banking, pharma, or commodities. Low relevance drives short sessions and low return visit rates.

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Industry benchmark (Netflix, Spotify) | Personalisation increases session time 2–3× |
| ET average session (baseline) | 4 minutes |
| Personalised session (estimated) | 8 minutes (+4 min) |
| Daily active users | 10,000,000 |
| Extra pageviews per user (2 pages @ 2 min each) | 20,000,000 pageviews/day |
| Ad revenue at ₹150 CPM | 20M ÷ 1,000 × ₹150 = ₹3L/day |
| **Annual incremental ad revenue** | **₹3L × 365 = ₹109 Cr** |

---

## Feature 3: News Navigator Briefings

**Problem:** Understanding a complex Indian business story (RBI rate decision, Adani Group saga) requires reading 8–10 articles across 3–5 days — roughly 30 minutes of effort. Briefings collapse this to 3 minutes: a 10× reduction in time-to-insight.

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| ET Prime subscribers | ~3,000,000 |
| Annual churn rate | ~25% → 750,000 churners |
| Briefings-driven churn reduction | 8% |
| Subscribers retained | **60,000** |
| Retained ARR | ₹999 × 60,000 = **₹6 Cr** |
| Premium B2B briefing API (500 corporates × ₹50,000/year) | **₹25 Cr** |
| **Total** | **₹31 Cr** |

The B2B channel targets law firms, investment banks, and family offices that currently pay Bloomberg Terminal rates (₹25,000+/month) for comparable India-specific intelligence.

---

## Feature 4: Story Arc Tracker

**Problem:** No Indian news product tracks the evolution of a business story with an entity knowledge graph, sentiment timeline, and AI predictions. Bloomberg Terminal provides some of this for global markets at ₹25,000+/month — entirely out of reach for most Indian professionals.

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Professional users in India (analysts, fund managers, journalists) | 500,000 |
| ET Prime Pro tier price | ₹4,999/year |
| Target: 2% of professionals | 10,000 users |
| **New premium tier ARR** | **₹4,999 × 10,000 = ₹5 Cr** |
| Competitive moat | No Indian news product has this feature |

Story Arc also increases stickiness for existing ET Prime subscribers — users who engage with arc timelines have higher intent and lower churn.

---

## Feature 5: AI Video Studio

**Problem:** Video production at ET requires a producer, editor, and graphics team. Cost per video: ₹15,000–50,000. This limits daily output to 5–10 videos and makes sub-3-minute explainers on fast-moving stories economically unviable.

| Metric | Baseline (Human) | AI Studio |
|---|---|---|
| Videos per day | 10 | 100 (10×) |
| Cost per video | ₹25,000 avg | ₹50 (API costs) |
| Daily production cost | ₹2,50,000 | ₹5,000 |
| **Annual production cost** | **₹91 Cr** | **₹18L** |
| **Annual cost saving** | | **~₹90 Cr** |

| Revenue upside | Value |
|---|---|
| 10× video volume → 10× YouTube/social reach | — |
| Conservative additional ad revenue from 10× content | ₹20 Cr/year |
| **Total (saving + new revenue)** | **₹110 Cr** |

## Summary

| Feature | Impact Type | Annual Value |
|---|---|---|
| Vernacular Engine | New subscriber ARR | ₹70 Cr |
| Personalised Feed | Incremental ad revenue | ₹109 Cr |
| News Navigator | Churn reduction + B2B API | ₹31 Cr |
| Story Arc Tracker | New premium tier ARR | ₹5 Cr |
| AI Video Studio | Cost saving + content revenue | ₹110 Cr |
| **Total** | | **₹325 Cr (~$39M USD)** |

At an estimated total API cost of under ₹5 Cr/year across all five features, the platform delivers roughly **65× return on AI infrastructure spend**.

---

## Assumptions

- All conversion rates are conservative (1–2% of addressable audience)
- API costs calculated at March 2026 OpenAI pricing (GPT-4o: $5/1M input tokens, TTS: $15/1M chars)
- Infrastructure scaling costs estimated at ~20% overhead (not deducted from figures above)
- Revenue figures are gross; net margin impact will be lower
- Market size and language preference data from IAMAI India Internet Report 2025
- ET readership and Prime subscriber figures from publicly reported ET Group media kit data
- Session time and personalisation uplift benchmarks from Nielsen and McKinsey digital media studies
